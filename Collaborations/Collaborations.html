üåê Opportunities for Collaboration

We welcome partnerships and collaborations with institutions, researchers, and organizations that share our vision for advancing physical education, sports science, and wellness. Whether you're looking to collaborate on academic programs, research initiatives, or community outreach, we offer a variety of opportunities to work together.

Collaboration Areas:

  • Joint research and development in sports science and physical education.
  • Curriculum development and faculty exchange programs.
  • Organizing conferences, workshops, and seminars.
  • Community outreach initiatives for rural sports promotion.

If you are interested in exploring collaboration opportunities, please feel free to reach out to us.

If you are an institution, researcher, or organization interested in collaborating with us, we encourage you to submit a detailed proposal outlining your objectives and the potential areas of collaboration. We are committed to evaluating all proposals with great care and aligning them with our mission and vision.

Proposal Submission Guidelines:

  1. Download the Proposal Template Download Link
  2. Complete the template with relevant details about your organization and proposed collaboration.
  3. Submit the completed proposal to collaborations@dhyansport.org.
  4. Please include any supporting documents, such as letters of intent, academic background, or previous work in related areas.
