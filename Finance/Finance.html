As a non-profit organization, the DhyanSport Sports & Physical Education Foundation is fully committed to meeting all legal and regulatory requirements for operating in India.

Section 12A

The Foundation is registered under Section 12A of the Income Tax Act, allowing it to avail of certain tax exemptions.

Section 80G

Contributions to the Foundation are eligible for 80G deductions, ensuring tax benefits for donors who support our programs and initiatives.

The Foundation complies with the FCRA for accepting foreign donations, as required for NGOs operating in India
