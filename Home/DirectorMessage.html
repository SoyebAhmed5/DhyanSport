Welcome Message from the Director

Dear Scholars, Students, and Sports Enthusiasts,

Welcome to the Dhyansport Institute of Sports & Physical Education Research—a premier academic and research hub dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in sports science, physical education, wellness, and allied disciplines.

As the Director, it is both my professional responsibility and personal honor to lead this institute, which operates under the aegis of the DhyanSport Sports & Physical Education Foundation, a Section 8 non-profit organization. This institute is a living tribute to my late father, whose principles of discipline, knowledge, and service continue to guide my journey. His lifelong commitment to education and integrity fuels the very mission of Dhyansport—to build a stronger, healthier, and more aware society through evidence-based physical education and sport.

The Institute is committed to:

  • Offering cutting-edge certificate, diploma, and advanced-level courses aligned with national and international standards;
  • Promoting interdisciplinary research and scholarly innovation through the Indian Journal of Sports Studies (ISSN 2229-3558);
  • Fostering thought leadership and collaboration through our professional bodies—the Indian Society of Sports Scientists and the Indian Association of Sports Anthropometry;
  • Serving the nation by nurturing talent, enhancing grassroots sports development, and contributing to public health and well-being.

At Dhyansport, we bridge tradition and technology, fieldwork and theory, rural and global perspectives—creating an inclusive platform for future educators, researchers, fitness professionals, and change-makers.

Whether you are here to learn, teach, research, or partner with us—we welcome you. Let us move forward together in the spirit of excellence, empathy, and empowerment through physical education.

Warm regards,
Dr. Tushar Dhar Shukla
Director
Dhyansport Institute of Sports & Physical Education Research
