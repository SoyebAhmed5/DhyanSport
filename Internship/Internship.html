Internship

At the DhyanSport Sports & Physical Education Foundation, we offer a wide range of internship programs and career opportunities designed to empower individuals and provide hands-on experience in the fields of sports science, physical education, wellness, and community outreach. Whether you are a student, a professional, or someone passionate about making a difference, we invite you to explore the opportunities with us.

Internship Programs

Our internship programs provide students and young professionals with an immersive experience in sports education, research, and community development. Interns will have the opportunity to work alongside experts, contribute to meaningful projects, and gain valuable insights into the industry.

Key Areas for Internships:

  • Sports Science Research
  • Physical Education Curriculum Development
  • Event Management & Sports Administration
  • Community Sports Outreach
  • Wellness & Fitness Programs
  • ports Journalism & Media

Benefits of Interning with Us:

  • Hands-on experience in a leading organization.
  • Mentorship from industry experts.
  • Networking opportunities with professionals and institutions.
  • Exposure to cutting-edge research and trends in the sports industry.

💸 Stipend Opportunities

We recognize the value of our interns' contributions and offer stipend opportunities for selected positions. The stipends are designed to support your experience with us while enabling you to focus on gaining knowledge and skills in your chosen field.

Stipend Amounts:

The stipend amount may vary based on the role, duration, and qualifications of the applicant.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Volunteer & Staff Recruitment

In addition to internships, we also offer volunteer and staff recruitment opportunities for individuals looking to make a direct impact in the field of sports and physical education. Whether you are seeking to gain experience or are a seasoned professional looking to contribute your expertise, we welcome your participation.

Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Event Coordinators for workshops, seminars, and conferences.
  • Outreach Volunteers for rural sports development initiatives.
  • Content Creators for research publications and social media.
  • Sports Coaches for grassroots sports programs.

Staff Recruitment:

We are constantly looking for talented and passionate individuals to join our team. Explore career opportunities in various fields, such as:

  • Sports Science Research
  • Program Management
  • Marketing & Communications
  • Administration & Operations
  • Events & Conferences

📄 Application Portal

Interested candidates can apply for internships, volunteer roles, or full-time positions through our Application Portal. Simply fill out the online application form and submit your resume and any additional documents required for the position you're interested in.

Application Deadlines:

  1. visit the Application Portal 👉 [Apply Now Link]
  2. Select the position or internship you wish to apply for.
  3. Fill out the application form with your details.
  4. Upload your resume and relevant docuents (cover letter, academic transcripts, etc.).
  5. Submit your application.
