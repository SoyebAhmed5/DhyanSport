Membership

Join the DhyanSport Sports & Physical Education Foundation and become part of a national network committed to excellence in sports science, physical education, and wellness.

Membership Categories

General Membership

For individuals: students, educators, trainers, and professionals.

Duration Fee(INR)
1 Year ₹2,500
3 Years ₹4,000

Life Membership

For individuals seeking long-term association. this is life time membership

Duration Fee(INR)
Lifetime ₹11000

Institutional Membership

For schools, universities, colleges, research centers, NGOs, and academies.

Duration Fee(INR)
1 Year ₹10,000

🎁Benefits of Membership

  • 🗳️ Voting rights (Life and Institutional Members only)
  • 📘 Free/discounted access to the Indian Journal of Sports Studies (IJSS)
  • 💼 Discounts on FDPs, MDPs, and academic conferences
  • 🧠 Networking opportunities with top professionals and researchers
  • 📥 Exclusive access to research archives, resources, and job updates
  • 🎖️ Eligibility for awards, fellowships, and speaker opportunities
