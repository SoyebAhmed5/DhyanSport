Academic Programs Overview

The DhyanSport Sports & Physical Education Foundation offers a diverse range of academic activities aimed at promoting excellence in sports science, physical education, wellness, and indigenous games. These programs are designed to cater to the needs of students, educators, athletes, and researchers, ensuring holistic development and a deep understanding of both traditional and contemporary practices.

üéìFlexible Learning: Online | Hybrid

At DhyanSport Sports & Physical Education Foundation, we are committed to making high-quality sports and physical education accessible to learners across diverse regions and lifestyles. Our programs are designed with flexibility at their core, enabling you to learn anytime, anywhere‚Äîwithout compromising on academic quality.

Online Learning

  • 100% virtual classes delivered through a modern Learning Management System (LMS).
  • Live sessions, recorded lectures, digital reading materials, and online assessments.
  • Best suited for working professionals, remote learners, and those seeking certifications from home.

Hybrid Learning

  • A balanced model combining online theoretical modules with periodic in-person practical sessions or bootcamps.
  • Allows hands-on experience in coaching, fitness training, or research-based activities while maintaining schedule flexibility.
  • Ideal for coaches, trainers, and educators looking to upskill without career disruption.
